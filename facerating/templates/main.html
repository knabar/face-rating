Face Rating Study 1

In this online study you will be examining several arrays of faces. There are three parts to this study, and the overall study should last about 30-45 minutes. You are encouraged to tell your friends to participate in this study!

Proceeding in this study by clicking "NEXT" below constitutes your provision of an informed consent. This means that you understand that the responses you provide will become the property of the researchers conducting this study. These responses will be kept anonymous and will not be tied to personally identifying information about you.

Participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may choose to end at any time. You will not receive any compensation for your participation. At the end of the study, we will provide you a debriefing that tells you more about what questions we are investigating.

There are several qualifications you must meet to be able to participate in this study:

    1. You must have a valid university email address ending in ".edu"
    2. You must NOT have been a student at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign enrolled in Psychology 100 at any time during the Fall 2006-Spring 2007 academic year.
    3. You must have a working internet connection and computer that allows the presentation of pictures or photographs on your monitor display. Mozilla Firefox is the preferred browser.

If you have questions or comments about this study, you may contact the researcher, Dr. Matthew R Lee, at leemr@jmu.edu at James Madison University.
