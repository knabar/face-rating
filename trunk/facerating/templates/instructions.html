Face Rating Study 1

Instructions

There are three parts to this study.

In Part 1, you will be rating a series of faces based on how prototypical you think the faces are of Asian American faces. This part of the study should take about 5-15 minutes.

In Part 2, you will be rating pairs of faces in terms of how similar you think the faces appear to be to each other. This part of the study should take about 5-15 minutes.

In Part 3, we will ask you to tell us what information you used to make your judgments. This part of the study should last about 5 minutes.

Finally, we will ask for some of your demographic information, after which we will debrief you about the project. You will be provided some email addresses if you have further questions about the study.
